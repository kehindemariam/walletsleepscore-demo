7 Metrics

How it's scored

Each metric is 0-100. The overall score is a weighted geometric mean — a single 0 forces the overall to 0.

Recency, frequency, balance, and last-active are fetched live from rpc.pharos.xyz via JSON-RPC. The other 4 (gas, dust, diversity, contract exposure) are synthesized in the browser from the wallet's transaction history and recent network state — they approximate what the CLI tool computes with a 2000-block scan.

Labels: WIDE AWAKE (90-100) · HEALTHY (70-89) · DROWSY (50-69) · LIGHT SLEEPER (30-49) · DEEP SLEEPER (10-29) · COMATOSE (0-9).
